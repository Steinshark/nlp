250 ml warm milk. 60g sugar. 7 g dry yeast. Mix everything well. 2 chicken eggs. I have large chicken eggs with two yolks. 30 g soft butter. Mix everything well. Add 30 ml vegetable oil. 1 teaspoon salt. 1 teaspoon 9% vinegar. Mix everything together. Sift 600 g flour. Knead the dough with a spatula. Add flour in parts. The amount of flour depends on the weight of the chicken eggs. Sprinkle the table with flour. Place the dough on the table. Knead the dough with your hands. Form the dough into a ball. Grease a bowl with vegetable oil. Place the dough in a bowl. Cover the dough with cling film. Let the dough rise in a warm place for 1 hour. The dough turned out well. Sprinkle the table with flour. Place the dough on the table. Divide the dough into 6 parts. Form each piece of dough into a ball. Cover the dough balls with cling film. Leave the dough on the work surface for 10 minutes. Sprinkle the table with flour. Roll out the dough ball with a rolling pin. Roll the dough into a roll. Trim the edges of the dough. Form the remaining dough into a ball. Make slits in the dough. Roll the dough into a roll. Press the dough down with a wooden stick. Form a bun. Do the same with the remaining dough. Repeat after me. It is so easy. Place the rolls on a baking sheet lined with baking paper. Cover the buns with cling film. Leave the rolls in a warm place for 30 minutes. Brush the rolls with a mixture of egg yolk and 1 tablespoon milk. Sprinkle the buns with white sesame seeds. Bake the rolls in an oven preheated to 180degC for 20-25 minutes. The browned rolls turned out great! You are so beautiful! Very soft and delicious! These rolls are so easy to make! Be sure to make these rolls! You will love this easy recipe!
